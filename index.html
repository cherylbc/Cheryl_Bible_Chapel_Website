Welcome to Cheryl Bible Chapel

We are a Christian Church teaching and practicing New Testament principles. The believers who gather to the Lord Jesus Christ at Cheryl Bible Chapel are committed to the true worship of God, to the equipping of Christians for holy life and service, and to the proclaimation of the good news of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

Meeting Schedule

  • Sunday Worship / Communion: 9:00am
  • Sunday Bible Classes: 10:00am
  • Sunday Family Bible Hour: 11:15am
  • Wednesday Prayer / Share / Devotion: 7:00pm
  • Friday Night Fellowship: Last Friday of each month at 6:30pm

Statement of Faith

  • Diety of Christ
  • Inerrancy of Old and New Testament
  • Imminent Pre-Millenial Return of Christ
  • Salvation by Grace Through Faith in Christ
  • All Sufficiency of Blood of Christ to Cover All Sin
  • Good Works and Godly Character Result From Indwelling Spirit of Christ
